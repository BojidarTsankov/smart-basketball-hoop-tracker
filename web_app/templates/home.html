How It Works

Shoot the Ball

The player takes a basketball shot during training.

Sensor Detects the Shot

Infrared sensors mounted on the hoop detect the ball.

Data is Sent to the Server

Raspberry Pi processes the data and sends it to Django Server.

Statistics Are Generated

The website updates your statistics and performance charts.
